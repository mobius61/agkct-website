Anuradha Giridhar Kamat Charitable Trust

To serve the needs of the underserved in Society

Mission

To promote sustainable and ethical living, to comfort the afflicted, and to serve as a beacon of hope for those in need of a helping hand in their hour of distress.

Objectives

To function as a non profit entity for philanthropy and charitable activites.

To assist and provide support for the needs of the community in the field of education, health, food & shelter, finance, and the environment.

1. Education – Support and facilitate educational initiatives.

2. Health – Provide assistance to people in need of medical and/or ambulance services.

3. Shelter – Support initiatives and institutions to provide food and shelter for destitutes and individuals in need.

4. Financial aid – administer and arrange for short term loans ranging from 1 month to a year for individuals and families in need of such help.

5. Environment - Promote a socialist model for sustainable living and the betterment of society.

6. Animal Care - Support for the rescue, rehabilitation, and welfare of animals.

Organizational Structure (Democracy at Work)

The organization is structured as a worker cooperative (worker collective) wherein each full time worker employed by the Trust will be a stakeholder and participate in decision making as to the direction in which to navigate the Trust activities, planning of the budget, and the allocation of resources.
